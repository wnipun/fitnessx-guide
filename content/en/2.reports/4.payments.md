## Payments Overview

Get a quick overview of payments made by members.

![Payments Overview](reports/payments-overview.png)

### Widgets

- **Total payments**: Displays the total amount of payments received.

### Filters

Use the filters at the top to customise the data displayed in the overview. You can filter by date range, specific members, or payment types.
![Payments Filters](reports/payments-filters.png)

### Exporting Data

::warning
This feature may require you to be on a specific subscription plan.
::

You can export the payments data by selecting all the entries. The data will be downloaded in CSV or Excel format for further analysis.

![Export Payments Data](reports/payments-export.png)

>Remember to select all entries if you want to export the complete data set.

#### Downloading the file

Once you click the "Export" button, the system will prepare the file. After a few moments, a download link will appear on the notification pane. Notification pane can be accessed by clicking the bell icon on the top right corner.

![Download Payments File](reports/payments-download.png)
