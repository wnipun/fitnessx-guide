## Attendance Overview

Get a quick overview of attendance trends for both members and staff. 

![Attendance Overview](reports/attendance-overview.png)

### Widgets

- **Today's member attendance**: Displays the total number of check-ins for the day.

### Filters

Use the filters at the top to customise the data displayed in the overview. You can filter by date range, specific members or permitted devices.
![Attendance Filters](reports/attendance-filters.png)

::note
Permitted devices filter will show users whose membership is associated with the selected devices. This will ignore user attempting to authenticate from non-permitted devices.
::

### Exporting Data

::warning
This feature may require you to be on a specific subscription plan.
::

You can export the attendance data by selecting all the entries. The data will be downloaded in CSV or Excel format for further analysis.

![Export Attendance Data](reports/attendance-export.png)

>Remember to select all entries if you want to export the complete data set.

#### Downloading the file

Once you click the "Export" button, the system will prepare the file. After a few moments, a download link will appear on the notification pane. Notification pane can be accessed by clicking the bell icon on the top right corner.

![Download Attendance File](reports/attendance-download.png)
