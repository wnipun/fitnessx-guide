## Creating Members
To add a new member, navigate to the "Members" section and click on the "New Member" button. Fill in the required details such as given names, last name, email, and membership type, then save the information to create the member profile.

## Member List
The member list displays all registered members along with their details, including image, member id, name, mobile number and status. You can search using name, member id or phone number to find specific members quickly.
