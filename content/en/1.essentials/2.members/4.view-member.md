## Member Profile
The member profile page provides detailed information about a specific member. You can view and edit their personal details, membership information, and payment history.

![Member Profile](essentials/member-profile.png)

### Widgets

At the top of the member profile page, you will find several widgets that provide quick insights:
- **Days remaining on membership**: Displays the number of days left before the member's membership expires.
- **Member ID**: Shows the unique identifier assigned to the member.

### Actions

The actions menu allows you to perform various operations related to the member, such as syncing member data from/to devices, enabling/disabling member access.

::note
Re-sync to primary device action can be found under the additional actions. In case the member data is not syncing properly, you can use this action to re-sync the member data to the primary device.
::

### Notifications

Depending on your subscription plan, you may have the option to send email or message notifications to the member. Ensure to toggle the respective boxes if you wish to activate notifications for the member.

### Payments

The payments section displays the payment history of the member, including details such as payment date, amount, and payment method. You can track all transactions made by the member here. New payments can be added by clicking the "New payment" button.

![Payment Screen](essentials/payment-modal.png)

Creating a new payment will update the members access on the device immediately.

### Member Details

The member details section provides comprehensive information about the member, including:
- Mobile number
- Identity number
- Date of birth
- Emergency contact name
- Emergency contact number
- Gender
- Additional details

### Access 

The access section shows the devices that the member has access to.

### Cards 
::note
Available only if using access card feature.
::

The cards section list the access card assigned to the member. New cards can be assigned by clicking the "New card" button. Card number can be read from the device's card reader by clicking the device icon.

![Create Card](essentials/create-card.png)
