## Creating Members
To add a new member, navigate to the "Members" section and click on the "New member" button. 

::steps{level="3"}
### Fill member details
Fill in the required details such as given names, last name, email, and membership type.
![Create Member Form](essentials/create-member.png)

#### Notifications
Depending on your subscription plan, you may have the option to send email or message notifications to the new member. Ensure to toggle the respective boxes if you wish to activate notifications for the member.

### Save member
After entering all the necessary information, click the "Create" button to create the member profile.

::tip
It will take a few minutes for the member data to appear on the device.
::

### Register member face
Once the member is created, you need to register the member's face on the device. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the face registration process.

### Completion
After completing the face registration, click "Sync member data" button under actions to import the member face to our system and to sync across all devices.

![Sync Member Data](essentials/member-actions.png)
::
